SyncShed

An all-in-one utility application that synchronizes notes and reminders across pc and mobile devices

React Native TypeScript Firebase Expo

Problem

Developers and other cross-platform techies often struggle with managing and synchronizing their notes and reminders across multiple devices, leading to fragmented information and reduced productivity.

Solution

I built a mobile app that allows users to create and manage notes and reminders, with seamless synchronization across their pc and mobile devices.

Technical Decisions

  • React Native + Expo for cross-platform support and faster iteration
  • Firebase for real-time data synchronization and cloud storage
  • TypeScript for type safety and improved development experience

Challenges

One of the main challenges was ensuring seamless synchronization across different platforms and devices, while maintaining data integrity and performance.

Outcome

The app successfully provides a seamless experience for managing notes and reminders across devices, improving productivity and reducing information fragmentation.

Future Improvements

  • Add support for multimedia notes, allowing users to attach images, audio, and videos to their notes and reminders.
  • Implement dark mode support for improved user experience in low-light environments.
  • Enhance search functionality to allow users to quickly find specific notes or reminders.
  • Integrate with third-party calendar and task management apps for better workflow integration.
