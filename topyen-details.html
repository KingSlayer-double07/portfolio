Problem

My father needed a way to efficiently manage his inventory and calculate sales for his business, but he was struggling with manual methods and spreadsheets.

Solution

I developed a mobile application that streamlined inventory management and sales calculations for my father's business, providing a more efficient and accurate solution than manual methods.

Technical Decisions

  • React Native + Expo for cross-platform support and faster iteration
  • AsyncStorage for local data persistence
  • TypeScript for type safety and improved development experience

Challenges

One of the major challenges was ensuring the app was able to replicate the functionality of the existing manual processes without becoming too complex.

Outcome

The app successfully improved the efficiency and accuracy of inventory management and sales calculations for my father's business, reducing manual effort and minimizing errors.

Future Improvements

  • Add cloud synchronization to allow access to inventory and sales data across multiple devices.
  • Implement advanced reporting and analytics features to provide insights into sales trends and inventory performance.
  • Develop a user-friendly interface for easier navigation and improved user experience.
  • Integrate with accounting software for seamless data export and import.
